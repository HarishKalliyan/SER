Our Mission

To empower creators to express themselves and connect with their audience through audio. Our platform is designed to provide a seamless experience for both creators and listeners. We believe that voice is the most expressive medium and we are committed to helping creators share their stories, ideas, and perspectives in a way that is authentic and engaging.

Our Story

We were founded in 2019 by two friends who shared a passion for audio and technology. They set out to build a platform that would make it easy for anyone to create and share their voice with the world. Since then, we have grown to a team of over 100 people who are dedicated to our mission. We have raised over $50 million in funding from top investors and have been featured in publications such as The New York Times, TechCrunch, and Wired.
